Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Here is how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you register for an account (such as your name and email address) and the expense data you enter while using Spendly, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and allowing you to manage your financial records. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You can delete your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. No personal or financial data is shared with any third-party service. We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond as promptly as possible.
